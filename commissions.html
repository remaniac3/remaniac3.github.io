Commission Procedure

  1. Provide clear reference pictures, can be photos and/or hand drawn pictures.

  2. Rough sketches/drafts will be shown to you, changes can be made at this point and I will make max. 3 times of changes, if requested. Additional USD 2 per draft change will apply after this.

  3. If you decide to proceed, kindly make full payment. I will continue working on that once the payment has been received.

  4. Extra charges will apply for corrections after the rough sketch stage. Therefore, it is highly recommended to request changes during the rough sketch stage.

Delivery Time

Depends on the complexity of the commissions, but usually will take 2-3 days for one character (longer time for full body works).

Due to other commitments in my life, it might take longer than usual to complete your commissions. Therefore, please be patient and consider twice if your commission is urgent.
