How to connect to a Drawpile session?


1. Launch Drawpile. Then go to Session > Join...


2. In most cases, the session will be hosted in the public drawpile.net. Type the session name and look for it within the drawpile.net list. Click Join with your desired session selected.


3. Enter your desired screen name and click OK.


4. If prompted, enter the session password and click OK.


5. Enjoy your drawing session!


If you have trouble accessing the session, please contact the session operator (the one who hosts the session).
